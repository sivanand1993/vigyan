Acme Corp Employee Handbook (Excerpt)

Vacation Policy:
Full-time employees accrue 1.5 days of paid vacation per month during their first year,
up to 18 days per year. After one year, employees accrue 2 days per month,
up to 24 days per year.

Work From Home Policy:
Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week with manager approval.

Benefits Summary:
Health, dental, and vision plans are available after 30 days of employment.stream
