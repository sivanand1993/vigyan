Acme Coffee Maker User Guide (Excerpt)

Setup Instructions:
1. Place the coffee maker on a flat, stable surface.
2. Fill the water reservoir with fresh water.
3. Insert a paper filter into the filter basket.
4. Add ground coffee (1 tablespoon per cup) into the filter.

Brewing:
Press the 'Brew' button to start brewing. The coffee maker will automatically stop once brewing is complete.
If you wish to pause brewing, press the 'Pause' button. Press 'Brew' again to resume.

Cleaning:
After use, discard the paper filter and coffee grounds.
Wash the carafe and filter basket with warm, soapy water.
Do not immerse the coffee maker's main body in water.
